How to Play

  • Phase 1: Memorize. You have 60 seconds to study a 6x20 grid where each row has exactly 1 green card.
  • Phase 2: The Climb. The grid turns face-down. Start at the bottom (Row 1) and click the green card.
  • The Catch: One wrong move and the game is over. See how many rows you can clear!
